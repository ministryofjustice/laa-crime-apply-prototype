Apply for criminal legal aid

Use this service to apply for criminal legal aid. The application will be means tested.

Your client cannot have a partner.

They must be one of the following:

  • receiving a passporting benefit, including Universal Credit
  • not working
  • a pensioner

Use eForms for all other applications.
