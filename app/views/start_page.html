Apply for criminal legal aid

Use this service to apply for criminal legal aid.

You can only use this service if your client:

  • receives a passporting benefit, or is unemployed
  • is single or their partner is a victim, prosecution witness or co-defendant with a conflict of interest

Use eForms for all other applications.

Before you start

We'll ask you for your client's National Insurance number and the case unique reference number (URN).

If you cannot provide them, you'll need to answer additional questions and may need to submit additional evidence.
